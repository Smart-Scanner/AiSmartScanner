Why we built this

Most retail traders make decisions on incomplete data — looking at one chart, hearing one tip, reacting to one news cycle. The result: emotional trades, lost capital, and the slow attrition of a once-curious investor.

We built Smart Screener to flatten that gap. Every stock in the Nifty 250 is scored against the same 18 parameters every hour. RSI, MACD, EMA crossovers, ATR-based risk, delivery percentage, Bollinger position, volume divergence — all distilled into a single 0–100 score, with the underlying signals one click away.

No tips. No "guru" newsletters. Just a fast, opinionated tool that surfaces the highest-probability setups and stays out of your way.

How we work

We're a tiny team that trades the same markets we write tools for. Every feature on the platform started as a script one of us wrote to solve a problem in our own trading workflow — and was only added when it consistently produced better decisions.

"If we wouldn't use it, it doesn't ship."

That's not a marketing line — it's why the dashboard looks the way it does, why we don't have a thousand unused filters, and why the scoring formula tightens automatically when the market regime turns bearish.

Our principles

Data over opinions

Every score, every signal is computed from market data. No "expert calls", no paid promotions, no hidden agendas.

Speed matters

The dashboard loads in under a second. The scan runs in the background. You should never wait on the tool.

One account, one device

Fingerprint-based device binding stops account sharing. You pay for your access — and only you get to use it.
